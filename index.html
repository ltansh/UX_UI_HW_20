    Metamorphosis:

    North American Butterfly Association Website Redesign

  • Team Size + Duration: 5 x 3 Weeks
  • Deliverables: RWD Across Web + Mobile
  • Tools: Figma, FigJam, G-Suite, Zoom

    • Project Overview

    Our team embarked on a task to redesign the North American Butterfly Association (NABA) website, aiming to elevate engagement and establish trust in its impactful non-profit initiatives. Our mission was to craft an inviting, user-centric interface that seamlessly educated and engaged visitors, fostering donations and active participation. We seamlessly merged modern design, intuitive navigation, and informative content, presenting the saga of butterfly conservation in a compelling light. Our revamped site provided secure donation avenues and interactive elements, fortified by responsive design and robust security measures. This comprehensive approach expanded NABA's influence, attracted a diverse support network, and solidified its credibility, championing the cause of preserving North American butterflies and underscoring NABA as a steadfast guardian of nature.

    My Roles

    UX Research:

    In a cohesive team of five, I fulfilled a diverse role that substantially contributed to our success. Rooted in robust research, I orchestrated insightful user interviews and harnessed them to create vivid user personas. I translated data into valuable insights, guiding our strategic decisions.

    UI Design + Art Direction:

    Guiding our creative journey, I conceptualized user journeys, performed heuristics evaluations, and took the lead in redesign ideation. Utilizing Figma, I brought concepts to life through mood boards, style guides, wireframes, and prototypes.

    Project Management:

    Simultaneously, I took on project management duties, guiding our course, and meticulously compiled a comprehensive case study and persuasive presentation deck, artfully showcasing our innovative approach and its tangible outcomes.

    A Comprehensive Exploration:

    Our journey commenced with meticulous planning, channeling user interviews towards avid gardeners and dedicated non-profit advocates. Insights emerged, encapsulating user expectations for transparency, a robust mission, and a visually pleasing interface. Analyzed data revealed valuable insights, shaping the roadmap for user satisfaction. Concurrently, site vulnerabilities came to light, fortified by insights gleaned from similar non-profit platforms.

    Definition

    Transforming Insights into Strategy:

    The transformation began as user personas breathed life into data, infusing our designs with authenticity. Crafting engaging user journeys, we charted a path where users discovered NABA through butterfly gardening content, fostering a desire to actively support its mission.

    Ideation

    Fostering Creative Sparks:

    Collaboration thrived as we embraced the "I like, I wish, What if" technique. A Feature Prioritization Matrix sifted through these ideas, uncovering those with the most profound impact.

    Prototyping

    Converting Vision into Form:

    User flows and wireframes breathed life into concepts. A mood board set the aesthetic tone, while a style guide propelled us into mid-fidelity designs.
